# Software Services Agreement — Northwind Shop (CTR-SHOP-200)

This agreement defines the scope of the Northwind e-commerce platform between the Contractor and the Client.

## Clause 2.1 — Cart and Orders

Shopping cart management, order creation and order status tracking are in scope.

## Clause 2.2 — Payments

Accepting payments by credit card and debit card is in scope. Integration with at most 3 payment providers is supported. This item has an effort pool of 30 hours.

## Clause 2.3 — Product Catalog

Product listing, category filtering and product search are in scope.

## Clause 6.1 — Exclusions (Out of Scope)

The following work is **out of scope** for this agreement and is priced separately:

- Accepting payment with cryptocurrency (Bitcoin etc.) is out of scope.
- Third-party marketplace seller integration is not included in this agreement.

## Clause 7.1 — Maintenance

Bug-fix maintenance is in scope for 12 months after delivery.
