# Software Services Agreement — Meridian CRM (CTR-CRM-100)

This agreement defines the scope of the Meridian CRM delivery between the Contractor and the Client.

## Clause 3.1 — User Authentication and Session Management

The Contractor shall provide local username/password authentication, session management and password reset functionality within scope. At most 3 concurrent sessions per user are supported.

## Clause 4.2.1 — Reporting

Generation of up to 5 standard reports per month is in scope. Adding date and category filters to reports is also included. This item has an effort pool of 40 hours.

## Clause 4.2.2 — Report Export

Exporting generated reports in PDF and Excel (xlsx) formats is in scope.

## Clause 4.3 — Dashboard

Standard chart and widget components on the management dashboard are in scope.

## Clause 5.1 — External System Integration

Integration with at most 2 external systems is in scope. This item has an effort pool of 24 hours.

## Clause 7.1 — Exclusions (Out of Scope)

The following work is **out of scope** for this agreement and is priced separately:

- Single sign-on (SSO) and third-party identity provider (IdP / SAML / OAuth) integration is out of scope.
- Mobile application development is not included in this agreement.
- Real-time data streaming and push notifications are excluded.

## Clause 8.1 — Maintenance

Bug-fix maintenance is in scope for 12 months after delivery.
